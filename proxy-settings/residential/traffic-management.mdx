## I. Overview

The Traffic Management feature helps you monitor and control proxy traffic usage for both main user and sub-users, preventing overconsumption.

**Key Features:**

1. Traffic Reminder: Email notifications when balance is low.
2. Usage Limits: Set traffic caps for sub-accounts.

## II. Setup Steps

Log in to your B2Proxy dashboard, navigate to 「Residential Proxies」.

### Configure Features: Traffic Reminder

Purpose: Receive email warnings when traffic falls below a set threshold.

**Setup:**

Enter threshold (e.g., 10GB) in 「Alert Threshold」.

Toggle status to 「Enabled」.

### Usage Limits (Sub-Users)

Purpose: Restrict sub-user traffic to prevent overuse.

**Setup:**

Enter limit (e.g., 5 GB) in 「Sub-User Limit」.

Select unit (GB/MB/KB).

Click 「Confirm」.

<Note>
  Tips: Alert Threshold: Recommended at 10–20% of total traffic (e.g., 10–20GB for a 100GB plan).
</Note>
