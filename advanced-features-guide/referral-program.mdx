B2Proxy’s referral program lets you earn commissions by sharing our proxy service. Simply **log in to your account to get your unique referral link**, then share it with friends, colleagues, or your social circle. When they register through your link and complete an order, you can earn **up to 10% cashback rewards**, which will be automatically credited to your account balance. This article will guide you step-by-step on how to join the referral program, share your link, and start earning.

## I. How to Join the Referral Program?

### 1. Register as a Member

Register and complete your sign-up on the official [B2Proxy](https://www.b2proxy.com) website.

### 2. Get Your **Exclusive Referral Link/Code**

After logging in, go to Dashboard → Referral Program, then copy your unique referral link or referral code.

### 3.Invite & Earn

Friends register using your link or code and purchase any public plan (excludes custom plans).

### 4.**Earn Commissions**

For each successful order placed by your referred friend (including plan purchases or wallet top-ups), you will receive a 3%–10% commission.

## II. Commission Calculation Rules

Friends' Cumulative Spending (USD) and Commission Rates:

| **Cumulative Spending Amount** | **Commission Rate** |
| :----------------------------: | :-----------------: |
| ≤1,000                         | 3%                  |
| ≤10,000                        | 5%                  |
| ≤30,000                        | 7%                  |
| ≥ \$30,000                     | 10%                 |

## IV. How to Withdraw Commission?

Go to Referral Program → Withdrawal\
Choose withdrawal method: B2 Wallet / Cryptocurrency\
Enter the amount and submit the application (minimum withdrawal amount is \$50).
