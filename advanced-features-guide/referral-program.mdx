How to Participate?

1. Register as a Member

·Sign up at B2Proxy Official Website.

2. Get Your Referral Tools

·Go to Dashboard → Referral Program.

·Copy your unique referral link or promo code.

3. Invite & Earn

·Friends must:

  Register via your link/code.

  Purchase any public plan (excludes custom/enterprise plans).

·You earn 3%~10% commission on their successful orders (plans/wallet top-ups).

Commission Tiers

Friend's Lifetime Spend (USD) Your Commission

0–1,000    3%

1,000–10,000    5%

10,000–30,000    7%

≥ \$30,000    10%

Example:

·Friend spends 800→You earn \$24 (3%).

·Their total reaches 1,500→Next300 purchase earns you \$15 (5%).

Key Rules

√ Unlimited Rewards:

·No cap on referrals or earnings.

·Commissions auto-deposit to your wallet balance.

× Exclusions:

·Custom/enterprise plans.

·Wallet-balance payments.

·Refunded/chargeback orders.

Withdraw Earnings

1. Go to Wallet → Withdrawal.

2. Select method (bank/PayPal).

3. Enter amount (min. \$50) → Submit.
