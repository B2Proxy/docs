This guide helps users quickly master b2proxy's core features, including proxy setup, traffic management, CDKey operations, and advanced functions - suitable for both individual developers and enterprise clients.

## Core Proxy Types Overview

### Residential Proxies

- Features: Authentic residential IPs, pay-as-you-go billing, country/region selection available
- Best for: Social media management, price monitoring, and other small-to-medium scale operations

### Unlimited Residential Proxies

- Features: No traffic limits, daily/monthly billing, fixed IP locations
- Best for: Long-term high-frequency tasks like large-scale web scraping or multi-account management

### ISP Proxies (Static Residential)

- Features: Dedicated static IPs, high-speed stable connections, long-term availability
- Best for: Account registration, live streaming optimization, and other scenarios requiring high-reputation IPs

## 1-2 Steps Selection Process

### 1. Identify Needs:

Short-term/small-scale? Choose Residential (pay-per-GB)

Long-term/high-frequency? Choose Unlimited

Need fixed IPs? Choose ISP (Static Residential)

### 2. One-Click Activation:

Complete setup in just 3 minutes via your dashboard

## Advanced Features

- CDKey Distribution: Premium users can generate traffic keys for flexible allocation or resale
- Sub-Account Management: Allocate quotas and permissions for team collaboration
